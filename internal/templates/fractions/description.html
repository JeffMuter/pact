What is Pact?

Pact is a dynamic task management platform designed for relationships built on clear expectations and mutual accountability. It transforms the way managers and workers collaborate by providing:

  • Customizable Buckets: Organize tasks by category, project, or objective
  • Clear Requirements: Specify exactly what needs to be completed—be it word count, multimedia assets, or specific deliverables
  • Task Tracking: Real-time visibility into progress and completion status
  • Reward System: Incentivize work with customizable rewards and consequences
  • Secure Submissions: Workers submit completed tasks for manager review with encrypted storage

For Managers

Take control of your team or individual relationships. Pact empowers you to:

  • Create and organize tasks across customizable buckets
  • Define specific requirements (deliverables, deadlines, quality standards)
  • Review and approve completed work submissions
  • Provide feedback and track performance
  • Set up reward structures that motivate and engage

Use cases: Team project management, mentorship tracking, performance incentives, contract work oversight, and personal accountability partnerships.

For Workers

Stay organized and on track. Pact makes it easy to:

  • View all assigned tasks in one place
  • Understand exactly what's required before starting
  • Submit completed work with supporting materials
  • Track approval status and feedback from managers
  • Earn rewards for consistently meeting expectations

Benefits: Clear expectations, transparent progress tracking, fair evaluation, and tangible rewards for your effort.

Your Privacy & Security Matter

We take your information security seriously. Pact protects you through:

  • Stripe Integration: Secure payment processing without storing sensitive financial data
  • Encryption: End-to-end encryption for task submissions and sensitive data
  • Secure Storage: Your data is encrypted at rest and protected from unauthorized access
  • Privacy First: We never share your personal information with third parties
